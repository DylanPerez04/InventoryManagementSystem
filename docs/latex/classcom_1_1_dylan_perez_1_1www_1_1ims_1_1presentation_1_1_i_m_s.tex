\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
\doxylink{classcom_1_1_dylan_perez_1_1www_1_1ims_1_1presentation_1_1_i_m_s}{IMS} is the base class for all Inventory Management System queries used by a client to facilitate inventory stock and sales analytics for all in-\/store and online transactions with their business. An \doxylink{classcom_1_1_dylan_perez_1_1www_1_1ims_1_1presentation_1_1_i_m_s}{IMS} object encapsulates the means by which a client can add, remove, and navigate through their available--and unavailable--selection of products. In addition, the \doxylink{classcom_1_1_dylan_perez_1_1www_1_1ims_1_1presentation_1_1_i_m_s}{IMS} object directly handles client-\/to-\/customer transactions which interact with the stock of the client\textquotesingle{}s available selection, allowing the \doxylink{classcom_1_1_dylan_perez_1_1www_1_1ims_1_1presentation_1_1_i_m_s}{IMS} object to provide analytical data regarding the client\textquotesingle{}s sales.
